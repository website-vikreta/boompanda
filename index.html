About Us

BoomPanda is India's Coolest Student Community with a mission to develop creative and skilled individuals by filling the gap between Training in our Education System and the Requirements in real world.

BoomPanda helps Students to Socialize & Network among other Students in the country through various Events, Trips, Competitions, Workshops, Seminars, etc. along with providing various Learning & Earning opportunities.

What do students get?

Socialize & Network with Students in real life
Witness a fun-filled Education journey and enhance your Skills
Earn unlimited money by completing small Gigs and Task
Exclusive/VIP entry into the country's top events
Receive exclusive Offers and Deals from Top Brands
