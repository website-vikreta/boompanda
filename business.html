Why Us

BoomPanda connects brands to college students where they live, study, work and play. We can get your products and services right in the hand of Students and get genuine results. We enable brands to hire a distributed workforce and on-demand team for all business needs across India to scale rapidly. We can help you achieve your targets in a scalable & cost-effective way along with tackling executional challenges and extend your outreach in Student Community. We will help you to develop and implement media and marketing strategies that connect with the Student Community.
